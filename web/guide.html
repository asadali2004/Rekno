‚è∞ Understanding Decay & Strength

üîó Connection Strength

Every connection between nodes has a strength value that represents how closely related the concepts are.

Strong (0.8-1.0)
Medium (0.4-0.7)
Weak (0.1-0.3)

üìâ Connection Decay

Over time, connections naturally decay (weaken) unless reinforced, mimicking how memory works. Rekno applies 5% decay per day.

Initial:
1.0
After 1 day:
0.95
After 1 week:
0.70

üí™ Reinforcing Connections

Interact with nodes and their connections to strengthen them:

  • Clicking on connected nodes
  • Editing related content
  • Creating new connections
  • Regular review and interaction
